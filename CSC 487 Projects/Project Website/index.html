Gameplay

The game "Dreamland" is inspired by the story from H.P. Lovecraft. In a land of dreams and the unknown. Dreamland is a 2D roguelike with unlockables that will help progress through the dreams. Unique playthrough with random items, enemies, and traps.


Story

The game starts with our main character sailing the seas in uncharted seas. A storm brewed and causes the ship to get pulled under the sea. Our main character wakes up with the ship wrecked and her crew is missing. She finds her self in an unknown land where she must survive against all odds.
